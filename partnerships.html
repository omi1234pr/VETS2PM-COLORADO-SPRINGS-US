For Employers: Access to Job-Ready Veteran Talent

Vets2PM MPLW provides a streamlined pipeline of transitioning service members and veterans who are already trained and certified in globally recognized standards like PMP, Agile, and Scrum.

Why Partner with MPLW?

  • Reduced Time-to-Hire: Access a pre-screened pool of candidates tailored to your hiring windows.

  • SkillBridge Integration: We coordinate SkillBridge internships, allowing you to train and evaluate talent risk-free before hiring.
