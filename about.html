Veteran-Founded, Mission-Focused

We are establishing a fully operational Vets2PM hub in Colorado Springs, driven by a clear mission to bridge the gap between military service and high-value civilian careers.

Our Strategic Mission and Timeline

The Business Case

Colorado's military footprint generates 5,000–7,000 annual DD214s, rising to ~7,500 by 2026. This presents a critical market need for a centralized, accredited transition service. Our goal is to capture ~1.6% of this transitioning population in the first year.

**Projected Revenue (Year 1):** Between **$863,904** (low estimate) and **$1,007,888** (high estimate), based on student enrollment projections.

Key Project Deliverables (Q3 CY26)

  • Securing a physical facility in Colorado Springs.
  • Hiring local staff and certified instructor cadre.
  • Establishing initial employer hiring pipelines.
  • Executing partnership agreements with at least two key military installation TAPs.
  • **Launch of First In-Person Cohort:** July 2026.
