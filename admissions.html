Who Should Apply? (Target Customer)

Our program is designed specifically for military personnel seeking to transition into high-paying, professional careers in Project Management and related fields.

Transitioning Service Members

Active-duty personnel eligible for DoD SkillBridge seeking internships before separation from Fort Carson, Peterson SFB, or Schriever SFB.

Veterans & Spouses

Post-separation veterans and military spouses looking to utilize GI Bill benefits (Chapter 33) to upskill or re-career.

Career Changers

Individuals with military experience seeking formal PMP or Agile certification to enhance their resume and earning potential.

Primary Funding & Benefits Pathways

The **MPLW training program costs $8,999 per student**. We specialize in helping you leverage government benefits to cover the cost.

Post-9/11 GI Bill (Chapter 33)

  • Primary Funding Source: The Post-9/11 GI Bill is the most common and effective way to fund the entire MPLW training.
  • Benefits: Covers tuition and fees, and may include a monthly housing allowance (MHA) if eligible.
  • Action: We help verify your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) and submit the required VA enrollment paperwork.

DoD SkillBridge Program

  • Benefit: Allows active-duty service members (within 180 days of separation) to participate in training full-time at no cost while still receiving military pay.
  • Action: We manage cohort coordination and partnership agreements with local installations, providing approved training tracks.

Other Funding Options

  • Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA) Grants
  • Employer-Funded Training/Tuition Assistance
  • Military Spouse Scholarships and Corporate Sponsorships

Three Steps to Enrollment

  1. **Contact Us:** Reach out to our local Colorado Springs team for an initial consultation to confirm your eligibility and identify your desired career pathway.
  2. **Confirm Funding:** We verify your GI Bill eligibility or SkillBridge approval status.
  3. **Start Class:** Enroll in the next available MPLW cohort and begin your training! **First Cohort Projected Start: Q3 CY26 (July 2026)**
